# AI Research Trends

Weekly research reports by Kaleb Foltz tracking new trends, tools, and developments in the AI, data science, and statistics space.

## Report Structure

Each weekly report covers:
- **New Tools & Frameworks** — Notable releases and updates in AI/ML/stats tooling
- **Research Highlights** — Key papers, findings, and breakthroughs worth knowing
- **Emerging Trends** — Patterns and shifts in the field gaining momentum
- **Statistics & Data Science** — New methods, packages, and best practices
- **Recommendations** — What to learn, adopt, or watch closely this week

## Reports

Reports are saved to `reports/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
